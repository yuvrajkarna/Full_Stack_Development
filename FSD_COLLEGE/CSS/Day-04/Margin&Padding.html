Maggi Reciepe

Ingredients:
- 1 pack of Maggi noodles
- 2 cups of water
- Maggi masala (included in the pack)

Instructions:
Step-01. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan.
Step-02. Add Maggi noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
Step-03. Mix in the Maggi masala.
Step-04. Serve hot and enjoy!

Optional: Add vegetables or seasoning as desired.

Tea Reciepe

Ingredients:
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup milk
- 1 or 2 teaspoons of loose tea leaves or tea bags (adjust to your preference)
- Sugar (to taste)

Instructions:
step-01: In a saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil and add 1 or 2 teaspoons of tea bags.
step-02: Let the tea simmer for 2-3 minutes on medium heat, depending on how strong you like your tea.
step-03: Pour in 1 cup of milk and add sugar according to your taste.
step-04: Turn off the heat, strain the tea into cups, and serve hot.

Optional: You can add spices like cardamom, ginger, or cinnamon for a flavored tea.

Omlette Reciepe

Ingredients:
- 2-3 large eggs
- Salt and pepper (to taste)
- 1 tablespoon milk (optional)
- 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
- Fillings of your choice (e.g., chopped vegetables, cheese, ham, mushrooms)

Instructions:
1. Whisk eggs with salt, pepper, and milk (optional).
2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
3. Pour egg mixture into the pan.
4. Add fillings on one-half of the omelette.
5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom sets and browns slightly.
6. Fold the omelette in half.
7. Cook for 1 more minute until fully cooked and lightly browned.
8. Slide onto a plate and serve.

Optional: Garnish with fresh herbs like parsley or chives.
